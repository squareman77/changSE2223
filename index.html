Effects of Exposure of Emotionally Affecting Media on Levels of Cognitive Dissonance Relating to Environmentally Conscious Behavior

Evan Chang 2021-2022

Overview

  • People partake in environmentally neglectful activities but have apparent support for environmental causes (recycling)

  • Cognitive dissonance is when person has contradictory beliefs/behaviors on issue

  • Intensity of cognitive dissonance depends on emotional importance of issue

  • Media w/ emotional appeal towards environmental conservation may increase cognitive dissonance intensity, causing belief adjustment towards environment issue
